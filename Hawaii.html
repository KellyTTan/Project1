Living in Hawaii

Hi, my name is Kelly. I was born and raised in Honolulu Hawaii.

Hawaii is a popular travel destination all year long and heres why:

Scenery

Hawaii is a great place to enjoy amazing scenery.

It's perfect people who love the great outdoors. There's many beaches and hikes to explore while you're here!

Weather

Hawaii is known for it's tropical weather all 4 seasons!

The average weather in about 80 degrees fahrenheit and it never snows.

Food

Locals and tourists love getting fresh poke bowls and garlic shrimp plates!

Hawaii is actually a very diverse place, so you can also find plenty of authentic asian food here.
